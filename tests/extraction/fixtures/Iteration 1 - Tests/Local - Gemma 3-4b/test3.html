Enablers for Agile Ways of Working

Transitioning to agile requires a multi-faceted approach. Key enablers include:

  • Agile Methods (Scrum, Kanban)
  • Agile PPM Tooling - Recommendation: Atlassian Jira/Confluence
  • Agile Coaching for Business and IT Teams
  • Agile Training Design & Delivery
  • Modern Engineering Support

The Importance of the Right PPM Toolset

Selecting the appropriate Project Portfolio Management (PPM) tool is crucial for successful agile adoption. A robust PPM solution provides:

  • Visibility into project dependencies
  • Improved resource allocation
  • Enhanced collaboration between business and IT
  • Better alignment with strategic goals

We recommend exploring Atlassian tools (Jira, Confluence) to streamline your PPM processes.

Journey to Active Agile

Implementing agile is not a destination, but a journey. Here's a simplified view of the process:

  1. Assess current state and identify gaps
  2. Define agile methodology (Scrum, Kanban)
  3. Implement Agile PPM Tooling (e.g., Atlassian Jira)
  4. Provide agile coaching and training
  5. Continuously monitor and adapt processes
