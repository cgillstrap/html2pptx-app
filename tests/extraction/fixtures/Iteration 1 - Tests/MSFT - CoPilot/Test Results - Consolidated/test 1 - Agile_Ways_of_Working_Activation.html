Activating Agile Ways of Working

Key enablers for sustainable adoption

  • Agile Methods Playbook Definition – a clear, shared playbook defining roles, ceremonies, artefacts and decision rights across Business and IT.
  • Agile PPM Tooling Approach – integrated portfolio, programme and delivery tooling to enable transparency, prioritisation and flow.
  • Agile Coaching for Business and IT Teams – embedded coaching to support behavioural change and practical application.
  • Agile Training Design and Delivery – role-based training to build common language and capability.
  • Modern Engineering Support – engineering standards, CI/CD and DevOps practices that enable iterative delivery.

The Importance of the Right PPM Toolset

Enabling agile planning, prioritisation and execution

  • Agile ways of working require end-to-end visibility from demand intake through to delivery and benefits realisation.
  • Traditional, stage-gated PPM tools limit agility, slowing prioritisation and reducing transparency.
  • A modern agile PPM toolset supports iterative planning, dynamic reprioritisation and alignment between portfolio, programmes and teams.
  • Recommendation: adopt the Atlassian toolset (e.g. Jira, Jira Align, Confluence) to provide a coherent front door for demand, delivery tracking and collaboration.
  • This creates a single source of truth across Business and IT, reinforcing shared ownership and faster decision-making.

The Journey to Activate Agile Enablers

From intent to embedded ways of working

  • Define and align – establish the Agile Methods Playbook and agree common principles across Business and IT.
  • Enable tooling – implement the Agile PPM tooling approach, aligned to the playbook and delivery lifecycle.
  • Build capability – design and deliver targeted agile training for leaders, product owners and delivery teams.
  • Embed coaching – deploy agile coaches to support teams, leaders and governance forums through the transition.
  • Scale and sustain – reinforce modern engineering practices and continuously evolve ways of working based on feedback and outcomes.
