Activating agile ways of working requires more than process change

Sustainable agility is enabled by a small number of reinforcing capabilities that reshape how technology and the business plan, prioritise and deliver together.

  • Clear agile methods that are consistently applied across teams and delivery types
  • Portfolio‑level visibility and prioritisation to align investment to business outcomes
  • Coaching and capability uplift for both IT and business leaders
  • Modern engineering practices that support iterative delivery at pace
  • Tooling that reinforces behaviours, not workarounds or manual governance

The right PPM toolset is foundational to agile delivery

Agile ways of working break down when portfolio governance, funding and execution are managed in disconnected or document‑heavy tools.

  • Supports a single, transparent view of demand, prioritisation and delivery
  • Enables iterative planning aligned to value, not fixed annual projects
  • Provides real‑time insight into progress, dependencies and constraints
  • Reduces manual governance overhead and status reporting

Recommendation: adopt an integrated agile PPM toolset centred on Atlassian Jira and Jira Align, complemented by Confluence for ways of working, standards and playbooks.

A structured journey to activate agile ways of working

Activation follows a deliberate sequence, building confidence and capability while avoiding disruption to live delivery.

  • Define the agile methods playbook – establish common language, roles, ceremonies and artefacts across delivery teams
  • Implement agile PPM tooling – configure the portfolio “front door”, prioritisation and reporting aligned to agile delivery
  • Deploy agile coaching – embed coaches to support teams, leaders and business stakeholders through behaviour change
  • Design and deliver agile training – role‑based learning for delivery, product and governance communities
  • Strengthen modern engineering support – align DevOps, environments and quality practices to iterative delivery
