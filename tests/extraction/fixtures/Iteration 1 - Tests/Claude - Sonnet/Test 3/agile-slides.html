02 Agile Ways of Working — Enabler B

The right PPM toolset is critical to agile success

Tooling connects strategic demand to sprint execution — without it, agile visibility breaks down

Why tooling matters

Without integrated project and portfolio management tools, agile teams lose transparency over demand, capacity, and progress. The method works; the benefits go unrealised.

The right toolset creates a single source of truth — from business request through backlog refinement to deployment — enabling shared prioritisation between IT and the business.

Critically, tooling must support both team-level agile delivery and portfolio-level visibility for leadership, without adding administrative overhead.

Recommendation: Atlassian suite — Jira Align + Jira Software + Confluence

Why Atlassian

Strategic layer

Jira Align

Enterprise agile planning tool — connects business strategy to portfolio, programme, and team execution with real-time roadmap and capacity visibility.

Delivery layer

Jira Software

Industry-standard agile delivery tool already in use at Glanbia. Highly flexible, supports Scrum and Kanban, and integrates natively with Jira Align — minimising change impact.

Knowledge layer

Confluence

Wiki-based knowledge management — the natural home for the Ways of Working playbook, sprint documentation, and decision records.
