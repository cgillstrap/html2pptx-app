2

Already Adopted Across the Organisation

Jira is well-supported and in active use across Glanbia IT, minimising ramp-up cost and leveraging existing institutional knowledge in the tool.

3

Flexible & Scalable Architecture

Configurable for lightweight agile teams through to complex programme governance. Scales from squad Kanban boards to full enterprise portfolio roadmaps.

Activation Journey: Phased Approach to Embedding Agile

Phase 1
Foundation & Design
Months 1–2
Agile AssessmentBaseline current delivery maturity, tooling gaps and team readiness across Business & IT
Playbook DesignDraft the Ways of Working playbook covering hybrid agile model, ceremonies, RACI and governance
Tooling ConfigurationConfigure Jira, Jira Align, Confluence & JSM to match the target operating model and demand process
Training CurriculumDesign role-based learning paths for Product Owners, scrum masters and business stakeholders
Enablers Activated
A – Playbook B – PPM Tooling D – Training Design
Phase 2
Pilot & Embed
Months 3–5
Pilot Sprint TeamsLaunch 2–3 pilot squads using the playbook and Atlassian toolchain on live business demand
Agile CoachingEmbed coaches with pilot teams, running sprint ceremonies, retrospectives and stakeholder demos
Training DeliveryDeliver role-based agile and Jira training to all pilot team members and business Product Owners
Refine & IterateCapture retrospective learnings to update the playbook and tooling configuration iteratively
Enablers Activated
C – Coaching D – Training Delivery E – Modern Eng.
Phase 3
Scale & Sustain
Months 6–9+
Organisation-Wide RolloutExtend agile ways of working to all IT product teams and aligned business domains
Portfolio GovernanceActivate Jira Align for enterprise portfolio management and strategic demand prioritisation
DevSecOps & CI/CDImplement automated testing, CI/CD pipelines and code quality gates to accelerate release cadence
Maturity MeasurementDefine agile KPIs (velocity, cycle time, NPS) and establish quarterly maturity reviews with leadership
All Enablers Active
