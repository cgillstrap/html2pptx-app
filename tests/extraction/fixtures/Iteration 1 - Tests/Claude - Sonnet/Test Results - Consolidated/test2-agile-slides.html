Why Atlassian wins on the key criteria

Scalability. Jira Align connects team-level sprints to enterprise portfolio roadmaps, giving leadership consolidated visibility without manual reporting.

Existing adoption. Jira is already in use in parts of the organisation — lower change burden and faster time to value than a net-new platform.

Native integration. Seamless data flow across Align, Jira Software, and Confluence eliminates the need to bridge separate tools via custom connectors.

Complementary tooling. The wider Atlassian ecosystem (e.g. Confluence for the Ways of Working Playbook) supports adjacent workstreams at no additional complexity.

  • Document agile principles and standards
  • Define sprint, backlog, and governance model
  • Publish on Confluence as living reference

Phase 1 · Weeks 2–6 B

Configure PPM tooling

  • Stand up Jira Align and Jira Software

  • Connect portfolio view to team boards
→
Phase 2 · Weeks 4–12 C

Deploy agile coaches

  • Embed coaches in priority product teams
  • Run sprint ceremonies alongside teams
  • Coach business product owners in parallel
→
Phase 2 · Weeks 6–14 D

Deliver training

  • Role-based agile training for all IT staff
  • Product owner bootcamp for Business leads
  • Certification pathways for key roles

Mature engineering

  • Introduce CI/CD pipelines per team
