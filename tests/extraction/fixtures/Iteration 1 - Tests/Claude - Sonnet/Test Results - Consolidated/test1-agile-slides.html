02 Agile Ways of Working — Enabler B

The right PPM toolset is critical to sustained agile delivery

A Project and Portfolio Management tool must bridge business demand capture through to sprint-level execution — giving leadership real-time visibility while empowering teams to self-organise.

Key selection criteria for Glanbia's environment:

  • Connects business strategy and priorities to team-level delivery
  • Supports both agile and hybrid delivery models
  • Provides portfolio-wide roadmap and capacity visibility
  • Integrates with existing SAP and engineering toolchain
  • Scales from team to enterprise without heavy configuration overhead
  • Low adoption friction — ideally already in use within the organisation

Recommended Toolset

Atlassian Suite

Jira Align + Jira Software + Confluence

  • Jira Align connects enterprise strategy to team sprints with combined roadmaps across all portfolios and real-time visibility
  • Jira Software is highly flexible — lightweight for simple teams, sophisticated for complex programmes — and is already in use across Glanbia IT
  • Confluence acts as the living Ways of Working wiki, hosting the playbook, governance docs, and delivery artefacts alongside Jira
  • Single ecosystem reduces integration complexity and accelerates adoption through a familiar user experience

Jira can additionally support change management workflows and the demand front-door, reducing the number of standalone tools teams must navigate.
