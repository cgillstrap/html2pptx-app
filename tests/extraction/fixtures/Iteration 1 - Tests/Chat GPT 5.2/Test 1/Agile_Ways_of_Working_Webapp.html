02 AGILE WAYS OF WORKING

Activating agile ways of working

Five mutually reinforcing enablers that drive transparency, cadence and delivery outcomes.

A. Agile Methods Playbook

Define the operating model: roles, ceremonies, artefacts, decision rights and governance (including hybrid delivery where needed).

B. Agile PPM Tooling

Enable end-to-end visibility from demand intake through prioritisation, roadmaps, sprints and benefits tracking.

C. Agile Coaching

Support Business and IT teams to adopt new behaviours, product thinking and ways of collaborating.

D. Training Design & Delivery

Role-based learning (PO/SM/Team/Leaders), practical workshops and continuous learning communities.

E. Modern Engineering Support

DevOps, CI/CD, test automation, quality and security guardrails to increase flow and reliability.

Why the right PPM toolset matters

Recommendation: standardise on Atlassian tooling to connect demand, portfolio and delivery.

What good looks like
  • Single “front door” for demand intake and triage
  • Shared prioritisation across Business and IT with clear decision points
  • Roadmaps and dependencies visible from portfolio → team backlogs
  • Reliable metrics (flow, predictability, value) and lightweight governance
  • Knowledge and standards captured once, reused everywhere
Atlassian toolchain (recommended)
Demand intake / front door Jira Service Management (portal + workflows)
Plan & execute delivery Jira Software (team backlogs, boards, sprints)
Portfolio alignment Jira Align (or Advanced Roadmaps for early scale)
Knowledge & playbook Confluence (WoW playbook, standards, reporting)
Design a single PPM “front door” experience for requestors.

02 AGILE WAYS OF WORKING

Journey to activate the enablers

A pragmatic, phased rollout that builds capability while delivering value early.

01Mobilise (0–4 weeks)
  • Confirm sponsorship, scope and success measures
  • Assess current WoW + tooling; baseline delivery metrics
  • Identify pilot products / squads and coaching needs
  • Define engineering guardrails (CI/CD, testing, environments)
02Design & configure (1–3 months)
  • Draft Agile Methods Playbook (roles, ceremonies, governance)
  • Configure Atlassian workflows, dashboards and intake “front door”
  • Role-based training (PO/SM/Team/Leaders) + communities of practice
  • Run pilots and iterate based on retrospectives
  • Implement CI/CD + test automation patterns
03Adopt & scale (3–6+ months)
  • Scale playbook and coaching across teams; lightweight governance
  • Portfolio visibility and dependency management; outcome reporting
  • Embed continuous learning and improvement cadences
  • Mature DevOps/SRE, quality and security automation
