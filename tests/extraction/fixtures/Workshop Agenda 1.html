From Reinvention Narrative to Investment Decision

CEO / COO Executive Workshop

Executive Workshop Objective

Align on the 1–2 AI-powered reinvention bets that deliver defensible margin expansion in Europe and authorize Phase 1 execution.

Why This Session Matters Now

  • Europe margin pressure is structural; integration complexity (incl. TNT) persists.
  • Competitive intensity rising (Amazon + digital-native benchmarks).
  • Capital discipline: cannot fund everything; need scalable, margin-accretive bets.
  • Readiness gaps must be closed before scale to avoid operational disruption.

Workshop Design Logic

Context Tension Solution Proof Commitment

By the End of the Session, We Will Have

  • Selected the primary reinvention bet (and defined a conditional second bet).
  • Agreed a funding envelope and stage gates tied to readiness KPIs.
  • Defined sequencing (Now / Next / Later) and key dependencies.
  • Established governance: sponsor, cadence, and 90-day milestones.
  • Aligned on Board narrative: impact, risk mitigation, and timing.

Decision Output

Clear commitment on Strategic Direction, Sequencing, Funding, and Governance.

Agenda & Decision Architecture

3-Hour Decision Workshop

Agenda

Segment Objective Decision Lever
Decision Framing Align on criteria, mandate, and what must be decided today. Define success metrics & decision rules.
Structural Case Establish urgency and Europe margin gap context. Confirm need to act + constraints (TNT, capital).
Economic & Sensitivity Review Validate financial credibility; stress-test assumptions. Agree “bankable” vs “speculative” value bands.
Feasibility & Risk Mitigation Surface readiness gates, integration risk, operational safeguards. Define gating KPIs + stage-gate funding logic.
Accenture Client Proof Build confidence via relevant client story archetypes. Neutralize “we tried this before” objection.
Capital Prioritization Compare initiatives; force trade-offs. Rank Primary / Conditional / Defer.
Decision & Commitment Lock direction and authorize Phase 1. Approve funding envelope + sponsor.
Governance & Next Phase Confirm owners, 90-day milestones, and Board update plan. Launch execution + operating cadence.

Objections We Will Address Head-On

  • “ROI assumptions are too aggressive.”
  • “Operational risk is too high.”
  • “Capital is constrained.”
  • “Data confidence is insufficient.”
  • “We’ve tried this before.”

Each objection is paired to an evidence segment, mitigation plan, and relevant client-story proof point.

Decision Moment

If we fund only one initiative this year, which delivers the most defensible 5-year margin impact?

  • Silent 1–5 scoring by each executive
