Design Principles

Principles that prevent KPI inflation and protect EBIT capture

Governance
  • Direct EBIT line of sight: Tier 1 KPIs must mathematically connect to last-mile cost/stop or cost/parcel.
  • AI embedded in decision rights: Measure where algorithms control routing + dispatch, not where analytics inform humans.
  • Network-level optimisation: Reward variance compression and density economics, not country local maxima.
  • Intelligence density over asset density: Track AI penetration, learning speed, overrides — not fleet size/headcount.
  • Leading + structural indicators: AI penetration, override rate, forecast accuracy predict margin expansion before EBIT moves.
  • Minimal KPI inflation: Strict subset of existing KPIs; only two structurally necessary new metrics introduced.

Must-Win Challenges & Roadmap

1Unified Last-Mile Data Spine

Barrier: Data fragmentation & signal latency

KPIs: Forecast Accuracy (D-1), Data Latency, Model Refresh Cycle

Failure signal: Forecast accuracy <85%; no intraday re-optimisation

Now what (0–90 days):

  • Single “plan vs actual” event schema across depots (common IDs: route, stop, parcel)
  • Define latency SLA by decision type (dispatch, re-route, exception)
  • Introduce refresh cadence KPI with automated alerts

2AI as Default Decision Engine

Barrier: Workflow embedding vs analytics layering

KPIs: % Routes AI-Optimised, Manual Override Rate, Route Adherence

Failure signal: AI >70% but override >25%

Now what (0–90 days):

  • Redesign dispatcher UI: “AI default” with reason-coded override
  • Introduce depot-level override league tables + coaching
  • Guardrails: OTD, safety, compliance thresholds prevent gaming

3Shift from Country P&L to Network Economics

Barrier: Local autonomy vs regional density

KPIs: Variance in Cost/Stop, Stops/Route, Miles/Stop

Failure signal: Persistent cross-country dispersion

Now what (30–180 days):

  • Define “network contribution” metric (mix + density normalised)
  • Introduce variance targets (Std. Dev.) alongside mean performance
  • Decision rights: where network can override local targets to protect density

4Incentive Realignment Toward Algorithmic Performance

Barrier: Cultural resistance

KPIs: Manual Override Rate, AI Adoption by Depot, Dispatch Productivity

Failure signal: High overrides in high-tenure depots

Now what (30–180 days):

  • Update scorecards: reward adherence + outcomes (cost/stop, OTD)
  • Separate “override allowed” categories (safety/compliance) from discretionary overrides
  • HR/ops joint governance: depot coaching plans tied to override metrics
